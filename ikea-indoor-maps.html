IKEA

Indoor Maps for the Great Megastore

Posted by Milan Markovic

You’ve heard of IKEA, right? The world-renowned household brand? They realized how important indoor navigation is from the start, and their interest in this area only grew stronger with the advancement of technology and digital signage.

They reached out to us to help them transform their floor signs and bring them up to pace with the current trends in indoor mapping. With the help of a little elbow grease, we turned their old wayfinding board into a fully functional digital solution using our own indoor map software.

PreviousNext

Images: Future Forward Brand Newmedia

Tailored with IKEA’s design philosophy in mind, the main application is installed on 3 large 42-inch touchscreens, which are spread over the store in Barendrecht, the Netherlands. These stands attract the attention of shoppers, who eagerly approach them to find and navigate the most efficient route in the IKEA store and quickly find the items they’re looking for.

Visitors can also browse the newest edition of IKEA’s catalogue and find out which special offers are available. They can even download the entire web app onto their mobile phones and simply walk away from the large touchscreen display with all the information still available at their fingertips.

Last but not least, in addition to providing timely and relevant information to its users, our digital signage solution also records which items IKEA’s customers are searching for. It supplies the store with timely and relevant statistical and analytical data and helps IKEA better understand their customers.
